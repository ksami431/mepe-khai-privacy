Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a meal using the AI camera?
Tap the camera icon on the home screen, take a photo of your meal, and our AI will automatically identify the food and calculate the nutritional values. Review the results and tap "Save" to log the meal.
How do I set my daily calorie goal?
Go to Profile → Settings → Nutrition Goals. Enter your target calories and macronutrients. The app will track your progress throughout the day.
Can I log water intake?
Yes! Tap the water drop icon on the home screen to log your daily water intake. You can set a daily hydration goal in your profile settings.
How do I view my progress over time?
Tap the "Progress" tab at the bottom of the screen to see charts and insights about your nutrition, weight, and habits over time.
I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link within a few minutes.
How do I delete my account and data?
Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. All your personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
The AI didn't identify my food correctly. What should I do?
You can manually edit any food entry after the AI scan. Tap on the food item, select "Edit", and search for the correct food or enter nutritional values manually.
How do I enable meal reminders and notifications?
Go to Profile → Settings → Notifications. Toggle on meal reminders and set your preferred reminder times. Make sure notifications are allowed for Mepe Khai in your iPhone Settings.
