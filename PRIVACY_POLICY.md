# Privacy Policy for Mepe Khai

**Last Updated: March 28, 2026**

## Introduction

Mepe Khai ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information that you voluntarily provide to us:

- **Account Information**: Email address, full name
- **Health & Fitness Data**: Age, gender, height, weight, goal weight, activity level, fitness goals
- **Dietary Information**: Food logs, meal photos, calorie intake, macronutrient data, meal times
- **Activity Data**: Step count, exercise type, duration, calories burned (manually entered)
- **Progress Data**: Weight logs, progress photos (stored locally on your device)
- **Notification Preferences**: Meal reminder times, notification settings

### Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent in app, interaction patterns
- **Log Data**: Error logs, crash reports, performance data

### Camera and Photo Library
- **Camera Access**: Used to take photos of meals and progress photos
- **Photo Library Access**: Used to select existing photos for meal logging
- **Note**: Progress photos are stored ONLY on your device and are never uploaded to our servers

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Services**:
   - Calculate personalized calorie and macro targets
   - Analyze food photos using AI to estimate nutritional content
   - Track your dietary intake and progress toward goals
   - Generate nutrition insights and statistics

2. **Improve User Experience**:
   - Send meal and log reminders based on your preferences
   - Provide daily nutrition summaries
   - Customize the app interface to your preferences

3. **App Functionality**:
   - Authenticate your account
   - Store and sync your data across sessions
   - Enable sharing of meal information

4. **Analytics and Improvements**:
   - Analyze app usage to improve features
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features

## Data Storage and Security

### Cloud Storage (Supabase)
The following data is stored securely in our cloud database:
- Account information
- Food logs and meal data
- Weight logs
- Activity logs
- User preferences and settings

### Local Storage
The following data is stored ONLY on your device:
- **Progress photos** - Never uploaded to servers
- Cached data for offline access

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption of data at rest
- Secure authentication using industry-standard protocols
- Row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data
- Regular security audits and updates

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access and Portability
- View all your data within the app
- Export your data in CSV or PDF format

### Correction
- Update your profile information anytime
- Edit or delete food logs, activity logs, and weight entries

### Deletion
- Delete individual entries (meals, activities, photos)
- **Delete your entire account** from Settings → Danger Zone
- All data will be permanently deleted immediately
- Cannot be recovered after deletion
- No waiting period for self-service deletion

### Opt-Out Options
- Disable push notifications in app settings
- Opt out of specific notification types (meal reminders, log reminders, daily summary)
- Disable AI analysis features

### Data Download
- Export your complete data history from the Progress screen
