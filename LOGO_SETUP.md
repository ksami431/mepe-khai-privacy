# Logo Setup Instructions

## Add Your Logo Images

To display your actual MK logo in the app, please save the logo images to the project:

### Step 1: Save Logo Files

1. **Icon Logo** (small circular MK on green background from Image 3)
   - Save as: `assets/images/logo-icon.png`
   - Recommended size: 512x512 px

2. **Full Logo** (bowl with MK and leaves from Image 2)  
   - Save as: `assets/images/logo-full.png`
   - Recommended size: 1024x1024 px

### Step 2: Where to Save

Navigate to:
```
/Users/samikhan/Mepe Khai - AI Diet Tracker/assets/images/
```

Drag and drop or copy your logo PNG files into this folder.

### Step 3: Reload App

Once the files are saved, the app will automatically reload and display your actual logo instead of the placeholder.
